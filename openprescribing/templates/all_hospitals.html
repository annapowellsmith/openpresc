OpenPrescribing Hospitals - coming soon

With the support of the NHS Primary Care and Medicines Analytics Unit we have started to build OpenPrescribing Hospitals. Every month, NHS England publishes processed pharmacy stock control data in the form of the [Secondary Care Medicines Dataset (SCMD)](https://opendata.nhsbsa.net/dataset/secondary-care-medicines-data-indicative-price). The [full technical specification](https://view.officeapps.live.com/op/view.aspx?src=https%3A%2F%2Fraw.githubusercontent.com%2Fpaulwestrip%2FOpen-Data-Portal-Documentation%2Fmain%2FSecondary%2520Care%2520Medicines%2520Data%2520%2520Release%2520Guidance%2520v003.odt&wdOrigin=BROWSELINK) of the SCMD is available to read, but briefly: it is hospital pharmacy stock control data, which is collected and processed by Rx-Info, and is now published on the NHS Business Services Authority website in the [NHS dm+d standard](https://www.bennett.ox.ac.uk/blog/2019/08/what-is-the-dm-d-the-nhs-dictionary-of-medicines-and-devices/). We have been spending time learning more about the data and carefully curating the data ready for use in OpenPrescribing Hospitals. OpenPrescribing Hospitals allows easy exploration of the SCMD through a set of curated measures and a custom analysis builder. We have used our domain knowledge to make exploring this data easier for a wider group of people.
